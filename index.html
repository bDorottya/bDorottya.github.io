HouseManager - My Bachelor's thesis

HouseManager - Real Estate webapplication

Used Technologies: Angular 13, Angular Material, MongoDB, Firebase

I worked about 5-6 months in this project and this is my first large-scale application which I built completely alone. There were 2 'versions' before the final one. At first I developed the app with a Nest.js backend and an Express.js middleware. The second 'attempt' used ASP.net as backend.

The users are able to search and save other people's properties or list their own ones. HouseManager has built not only for property listings, but for Experts in every housing-related fields. Users can sign up as an expert and can advertise their services with prices. People can write reviews about them and also save experts' contact info directly to their profile.

The app consist of features such as authentication, image upload, user profile, property profile with saving function, expert profile also with saving function.

The most challenging part of the building process was finding the best practice of connecting the Angular client app with Firebase and MongoDB. At the end phase of the final product, I should have refactor the models and functions, because throughout the development I realized I over-complicated most of them in the beginning.

I can say, that I learnt a lot about Angular, MongoDB, Firbase, serverless programming and Material design because of this app.
